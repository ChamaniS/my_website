Voluntary Experiences

My journey in voluntary work has been rewarding, offering me opportunities to contribute to the community and support meaningful causes. I have actively participated in various initiatives, focusing on education, social services, and community development. Some of my key experiences include:

  • Volunteer at AAAI 2025 (February 2025):
    Supported the AAAI 2025 conference held in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • Volunteer at NeurIPS 2024 (December 2024):
    Contributed to the Women in Machine Learning (WiML) Workshop at NeurIPS 2024 in Vancouver, Canada.
  • Reviewer of Research Papers (November 2019 – Present):
    • Journals: Computers and Electrical Engineering
    • Conferences: ICITR (2019–2021), ICPRAM 2019, ICIPRoB 2020, ICASSP 2023, MICCAI-DeCaF 2024, CVPR-WiCV (2023–2025)
  • Computer Lab Renovation – Weerasuriyakanda Junior School, Sri Lanka (December 2024 – March 2025):
    Pioneered a fundraising initiative to renovate a rural school's computer lab in Sri Lanka. Led the project in partnership with the Sri Lanka Humanitarian Foundation of Canada.
  • Mentor – ScholarX Program (May – November 2022):
    Mentored students through the ScholarX program organized by the Sustainable Education Foundation, Sri Lanka.
  • Delegate – AIESEC Summer Camp, Taiwan (July 14 – July 23, 2020):
    Represented Sri Lanka at the AIESEC summer camp hosted by National Taipei University for high school students.
  • Participant – Tea Industry Survey (2020):
    Participated in a tea industry survey for Sri Lankan tea companies organized by University of Moratuwa in collaboration with AIESEC GIP.
  • Editorial Member – Mora Mag & Media Unit (September 2016 – June 2018):
    Served on the editorial board of Mora Mag literature magazine and contributed to the University of Moratuwa media unit.
  • Editorial Member – Literary Associations, Devi Balika Vidyalaya (2007 – 2010):
    Member of the editorial boards of the Sinhala and English Literary Associations.

I believe that voluntary work not only strengthens the fabric of society but also enhances personal growth, providing invaluable experiences and skills. I am always eager to take on new challenges that allow me to make a positive impact.

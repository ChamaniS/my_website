Voluntary Experiences

My journey in voluntary work has been rewarding, offering me opportunities to contribute to the community and support meaningful causes. I have actively participated in various initiatives, focusing on education, social services, and community development. Some of my key experiences include:

  • Volunteer at AAAI 2025 (February 2025):
    Supported the AAAI 2025 conference held in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • Volunteer at NeurIPS 2024 (December 2024):
    Contributed to the Women in Machine Learning (WiML) Workshop at NeurIPS 2024 in Vancouver, Canada.
  • Reviewer of Research Papers (2019 Nov – Ongoing):
    • Journals: Computers and Electrical Engineering
    • Conferences: ICITR 2019, ICPRAM 2019, ICIPRoB 2020, ICITR 2020, ICITR 2021, ICASSP 2023, MICCAI-DeCaF 2024, CVPR-WiCV 2023–2025
  • Computer Lab Renovation at Weerasuriyakanda Junior School, Sri Lanka (Dec 2024 – Mar 2025):
    Pioneered the fundraising program to renovate a computer lab in a rural Sri Lankan school. Successfully led and completed the project in collaboration with the Sri Lanka Humanitarian Foundation of Canada.
  • Mentor – ScholarX Program (May – Nov 2022):
    Mentored students in the ScholarX program organized by the Sustainable Education Foundation, Sri Lanka.
  • Delegate – AIESEC Summer Camp, Taiwan (July 14 – July 23, 2020):
    Represented Sri Lanka at the AIESEC Summer Camp for high school students hosted by National Taipei University.
  • Participant – Tea Industry Survey (2020):
    Took part in a tea industry survey organized by University of Moratuwa and AIESEC GIP for Sri Lankan tea companies.
  • Editorial Board Member – Mora Mag & Media Unit (Sept 2016 – June 2018):
    Contributed to the Mora Mag literature magazine and the Media Unit at University of Moratuwa.
  • Editorial Board Member – Literary Associations, Devi Balika Vidyalaya (2007 – 2010):
    Served on the Sinhala and English literary boards at Devi Balika Vidyalaya, Colombo 08.

I believe that voluntary work not only strengthens the fabric of society but also enhances personal growth, providing invaluable experiences and skills. I am always eager to take on new challenges that allow me to make a positive impact.
